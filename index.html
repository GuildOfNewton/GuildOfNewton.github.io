Welcome!

Welcome to the RPG Guild of Newton! We are a role-player group with an emphasis on Dungeons and Dragons. We appreciate you being a part of the group and hope you have fun. We also appreciate your patients as we get things going. We hope to start meeting weekly in February 2023. We'll remind you as we get closer. Discord will be where all official communications will be, so check there often.

What to Bring

Generally speaking one doesn't need a lot of materials to play Dungeons and Dragons. If you're thinking about coming for the first time and have never played just bring yourself the first time.

The Digital Way

A smartphone, tablet, chormebook or laptop could take the place of anything one might need for Dungeons and Dragons. The D&D Beyond App can help you create and use your character sheet and roll dice for you. There are a number of other apps that help you play D&D as well. Make sure you bring a charger with your device since sessions can last four hours or longer.

While most everything can be done on a device, battles take place on a mat on the table. It is helpful to have a miniature of your character.

The Paper Way

Below is a list of basic items. One wouldn't need all of these necessarily, but they may be helpful.

Polyhedral Dice Set Dice the most important thing to have. Sets come with a 4-, 6-, 8-, 10-, 2 12-, and a 20-sided die.
Character Sheet While you could make your character in D&D Beyond and print out your character sheet, we don't object to the old school method here.
Miniature of your Character Most of D&D takes place in our imagination. However, battles typically play out on a tabletop mat and having a minature helps keep track of where your character is at. There are cheap ones to be acquired or you could make one yourself.
A notebook or journal While it isn't necessary it is beneficial to take notes on what has happened for future reference.
D&D Player Handbook While one can learn to play just by being a part of the group, it is helpful to have the player's handbook to read and reference

Code of Conduct

The RPG Guild of Newton wishes to provide a fun, safe, and harassment-free experience for everyone. Everyone is welcome regardless of gender, identity, sexual orientation, disability, appearance, body size, race, or religion. We do not tolerate harassment of any kind, in or out of the game. Participants violating these rules may need to leave.

The Dungeon Masters, or DM for short, are the ones in charge of the game at their table. They are there to facilitate gameplay and make the final decision on rules. It is up to the DM as to what rules they play with. Please, respect what the DM says and avoid game-stopping arguments. DMâ€™s need to keep the gameplay fun and engaging for all players.

The financial requirements to play are up to you the player. D∓D can be a very cheap game to play. You may use an existing mobile device like a smartphone/tablet or use paper and pencil. We do not want financial hardships preventing you from playing. If you cannot afford something, please, speak with one of the organizers. The organizers try to keep the costs as low as possible.

Play safe and clean. No weapons, real or props, are allowed on the premises. The only weapons allowed are in-game, imaginary weapons, or as part of a miniature of the character. This group is open to all ages. Please, use appropriate language and imagery for the youngest member at your table. Please, include all players while playing. Be respectful to each other and the DMs. The venue is allowing us to play here for very cheap. Please, make sure you leave it in as good or better condition as you found it.

You may contact an organizer or a DM to report violations of this code of conduct.
